Beauty

She is beauty, she is grace
But more than that her heart is lace
It's delicate but beautiful
It isn't hard to make it race
It rustles with the changing wind
But settles in its little space
tucked away and guarded closely
it stores a lot, but truly, mostly
wants to share its little secrets
with a soul that reacts kindly
it has its flaws, it has its scars
but always aims beyond the stars
so there is beauty, there is grace
and much more to a pretty face

hopeless

they call me a hopeless romantic
but i do not agree
i know there is a lot of hope
within this heart of mine
i love without regretting
what would've could've been
i love to think there is a love
that’s built and made for me
i love to love and be loved
i love to truly care
i love to give my whole full heart
while never being scared
so am i a hopeless romantic
for thinking things might be
as beautiful and pure
as i would have them be
